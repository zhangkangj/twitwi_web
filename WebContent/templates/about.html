About us

What is this?


  vs     on  

We monitor the 2012 Presidential election on Twitter. Currently, we measure how many times each candidate is mentioned on each day in each state and what topics are frequently mentioned.

Tweets are filtered based on their users and the presence of certain keywords. We follow approximately ten million active American Twitter users. These users are filtered based on their locations and connections. Thus we could remove most irrelevant tweets from spam-bots or foreign countries. A large set of keywords is used to track election-related tweets, and as of Oct 9th, there are over 50 million tweets since Jan 1st, 2012. Of course, with such amount of data, there are a lot more analyses that could be done. Suggestions are most welcome.
