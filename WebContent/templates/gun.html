Geographical and temporal distribution of discussions

We counted the tweets mentioning "gun" over a period from 12/12/2012 to 1/18/2013 and we then matched the users' locations in the US. Then we accumulated both mention counts and tweet counts by hour to create this visualization. The color corresponds to the percentage of tweets mentioning "gun" in a given hour. You can easily see how the nation responded to several important events along the Sandy Hook shooting timeline.

Resistance to stricter gun control

By classifying the tweets after the shooting, we look at the attitude towards greater gun control in each state. We found that disagreement with greater gun control is positively related to the percentage gun ownership and negatively related to the population density within that state.
